About Us

We are a team of scientists and engineers developing the autonomy stack for our robot.

Equipped with LIDAR and a ZED depth camera, the car's sensors will allow us to create various nodes in ROS2.

Check out our labs to see our wall follower, parking controller, and SLAM implementations, or videos to see our car in action!
